Munno AI Account and data deletion

Account And Data Deletion

Munno AI users can use this page to request deletion of their account and associated data, including data linked to WhatsApp assistant setup, reminders, and supported account integrations.

Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your Munno AI account and associated data, email us using the button below.

Request deletion by email

Email: privacy@munno.ai
Subject: Munno AI account deletion request

Information To Include

Include one or more of the following so we can identify the account:

  • The phone number linked to Munno AI.
  • The email address used to sign in, if account sign-in is enabled.
  • The WhatsApp owner number configured in Munno.

Do not send passwords, OTPs, PINs, mobile money secret codes, card numbers, or bank details.

What We Delete

When we confirm the account, we will delete or deactivate data we control that is associated with the Munno AI account, where technically possible:

  • Munno account record.
  • Linked phone number or owner number.
  • WhatsApp authorization records and session mapping.
  • WhatsApp companion session credentials controlled by Munno, where supported.
  • Reminders and reminder sync records.
  • Linked email tokens or account references, if email linking is enabled.
  • Generated document references or temporary file records controlled by Munno.
  • Server-side assistant records tied to the account, where present.
  • Support/profile settings tied to the account.

What May Remain

Some information may remain for limited and legitimate reasons:

  • Security, fraud-prevention, abuse-prevention, or audit logs.
  • Records required for legal, compliance, dispute, or platform-safety reasons.
  • Aggregated or anonymized analytics that no longer identify the user.
  • Data stored by third-party services under their own policies, such as Google, Microsoft, Apple, WhatsApp, AI providers, or cloud providers.
  • Data stored only on the user's device until the user clears app data or uninstalls the app.

If we must retain some information, we will keep only what is reasonably needed and only for as long as necessary.

How Long It Takes

We aim to acknowledge deletion requests within 7 days and complete eligible deletion requests within 30 days, unless more time is required for security, verification, legal, or technical reasons.

In-App Options

Where available, users can also:

  • Disconnect WhatsApp in Munno settings.
  • Remove Munno from WhatsApp under Linked devices.
  • Delete reminders in the app.
  • Clear local Focus Inbox data in the app where supported.
  • Disable Android notification access in Android settings.
  • Uninstall Munno AI or clear app storage to remove local device data.

Important Notes

  • This page is for deleting Munno AI account and associated Munno-controlled data.
  • It does not delete the user's WhatsApp, Google, Microsoft, Apple, MTN, Airtel, bank, or device account.
  • Users should never send passwords, OTPs, PINs, card numbers, or mobile money secret codes in a deletion request.
